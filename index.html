About Aftermath

Founded in 2020, Aftermath Art Studio emerged from the belief that art should challenge, inspire, and transform. We are a collective of contemporary artists pushing the boundaries of creative expression.

Our studio serves as both a creative workspace and gallery, fostering collaboration between established and emerging artists. We specialize in contemporary art across various mediums, from traditional painting and sculpture to digital installations and interactive experiences.

At Aftermath, we believe that art is not just about the final piece, but about the journey, the process, and the conversations it creates. Each work tells a story, captures a moment, or challenges a perspective.
